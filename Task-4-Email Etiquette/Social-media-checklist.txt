Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

✅ Do’s

✔ Think before posting – ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
✔ Protect your privacy – use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings
✔ Verify information before sharing – avoid spreading misinformation
✔ Maintain a positive digital footprint – post content that reflects your values and goals
✔ Respect others online – be polite and avoid cyberbullying

❌ Don’ts

✘ Don’t share personal/sensitive information (address, phone number, passwords)
✘ Don’t post offensive or harmful content
✘ Don’t believe everything you see online without checking sources
✘ Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling
✘ Don’t spend excessive time on social media that affects studies or health
